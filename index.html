About Us

Welcome to Saffrony Institute of Technology,or SIT,is one of the top engineering colleges in Gujarat, specifically in Ahmedabad-Mehsana. It is known for its commitment to providing quality education and fostering the holistic development of its students. SIT offers a wide range of diploma, bachelor, and postgraduate programs in engineering.The institute is affiliated with GTU and is approved by AICTE. SIT aims to nurture competent, socially responsible, and spiritually aware individuals.
